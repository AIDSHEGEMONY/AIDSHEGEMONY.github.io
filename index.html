What is AIDS HEGEMONY?

AIDS HEGEMONY is a avant-garder anti-cultural harsh noise and noisecore project. Was formed in Russia, Vladivostok in 2015. The band has its own unique "blowing your mind away" sound. The texts tell us about historical events, social problems and various sides of human nature.
